Poem 3: The one that broke the shelves

Back home?

Mood

Do you really matter at all?
Maybe we need to find out why is that
So that we could quest and go stall
Looking for the answer, out in the world we cant recall

Do things really happen in balance?
What about the things that happened no one solved,
Trapped in eternal imbalance
Still no answer, what if we couldn't find one?

Standards set at high disregard,
No one can achieve
No matter how one works hard into the night
We really can’t, there is no purpose other than hard.

Things never change, how hard you adapt
Why it happens, no one can draft
The only thing that one can do in these times of need
Is to help others that are around in wake up from their sleep.
What if I couldn’t even try to help them get up?
All hope is lost, the end is near, we have already given up

Why will no one ever see me?
maybe, im just a shadow working at the seems
Finding my purpose under the moon
What if no one finds me?
When I stray out too far from thee

Will I be able to survive?
After all these problems
And the too-high stuff I strive,
Maybe I shouldn’t but I do
I cannot really stop,
“As the potential is in me.”

Forever alone, always striving for the impossible
Never waking up from doing all of these impossibles
Still in there, soundly sleeping
Waiting for the banging ending
Doing whatever I can with materials at the seams

Play in tune with this
